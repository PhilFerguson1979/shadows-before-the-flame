# The Dragon Army

The Dragon Army is the military force of **Takhisis**, the Queen of Darkness, currently invading Ansalon.

## What We Know

- Commanded by **Lord Verminaard** (mentioned by Hederick under interrogation)
- Uses the **Seekers** as advance agents to subdue cities before the army arrives
- Seekers confiscate magical items and sell civilians into slavery as part of this campaign
- Dragons have been spotted on the horizon above Solace — the army is close
