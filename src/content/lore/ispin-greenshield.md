# Ispin Greenshield

A seasoned adventurer and trusted friend whose parting words set us on the path we now walk.

## Ispin's Final Letter

Delivered by **Otik Sandath** at the Inn of the Last Home, Ispin's letter spoke of unease that had followed him into his final days — a wrongness in the world he couldn't name but couldn't ignore.

He warned of:

- A strange *pulling* in the world, as if time itself were being forced backward by unseen hands
- A Shadow reaching out from an Abyss thought long sealed
- **Artifacts of ancient light** finding their way to those who need them — which must not fall into the wrong hands
- A fragile peace that may not hold

His words were a farewell, but also a call to action — sharp hearts, steady hands, and watchful eyes.

Along with the letter, Ispin left the party a **Robe of Useful Items** — his practical parting gift, held in trust by Otik.

## The Passing of Ispin Greenshield

Ispin Greenshield died of a sudden, violent fever — the "Shaking Chill." The High Seekers and local healers insisted he went quickly and ordered a swift, closed-casket burial to prevent the spread of the sickness. To the public, it was the tragic, natural end of an old traveler.

## Official Proclamation of the High Seekers

*Regarding the Passing of Ispin Greenshield*

> It is with a heavy heart and a prayer to the New Gods that the High Seekers of Solace announce the sudden departure of our long-time resident and traveler, **Ispin Greenshield**.
>
> Ispin was taken from us in the late hours of the night by a swift and merciless manifestation of the "Shaking Chill." While we know many of you held him in high regard for his tales and his jovial spirit at the Inn of the Last Home, we must prioritize the safety of the boughs we call home.
>
> **A Necessary Silence** — Due to the aggressive and contagious nature of the fever, the High Seekers have overseen a *private, swift, and closed-casket interment*. This was done under strict quarantine protocols to ensure that the "Shaking Chill" did not descend upon the families of Solace. We ask that the citizens do not gather near his former residence, as the property remains under a purification mandate.
>
> **A Life of Wandering** — Ispin was a man of the road, and it is the opinion of the healers that his many years of exposure to the uncivilized wilds finally broke his constitution. He died as he lived — quietly, between journeys.
>
> **A Final Warning** — Let this be a reminder to all: the world beyond our Vallenwoods is filled with sickness and uncertainty. Trust in the wisdom of the Seekers and the protection we provide. Ispin's worldly debts and affairs are being settled by the High Office; any inquiries regarding his estate are to be directed to the High Seeker's guards.
>
> Go about your business in peace. Solace remains safe under our watch.
>
> *— The High Council of Seekers, Solace*

## Legacy

Ispin could not be here to face what is coming. But his foresight, his trust, and his gifts continue to travel with us. His warning has proven accurate in ways we are only beginning to understand.
