# The Blue Crystal Staff

A staff of blue crystal carried by **Goldmoon**. It catches and refracts firelight in mesmerizing patterns.

## What We Know

- **Hederick the High Seeker** attempted to seize it by force — triggering the bar fight at the Inn of the Last Home
- Its significance is unknown. Why Hederick wanted it so desperately remains unclear.
- It appears to have some connection to the divine — possibly an artifact of the old gods
