# Inn of the Last Home

**Region:** Solace | **Status:** Standing (narrowly saved from fire)

Solace's most beloved tavern, carved into a massive Vallenwood tree. Run by **Otik Sandath**. Famous for its spiced potatoes and its perch high among the branches.

## Session 1 Events

- Site of the party's first major encounter.
- A grease fire broke out after Cogmaw killed Sergeant Hagar — nearly burned the tree down.
- **Tika Waylan** smothered the fire with flour bags, saving the inn.
- Hederick's guards were defeated here.
