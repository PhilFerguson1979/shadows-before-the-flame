# Otik Sandath

**Role:** Innkeeper, Inn of the Last Home | **Disposition:** Friendly

Stout, round-shouldered, flour-dusted apron. Famous for his **spiced potatoes**.

## What We Know

Longtime proprietor of the Inn of the Last Home. Knew Ispin Greenshield and held his final letter and the Robe of Useful Items until the party arrived.

## Session 1 Events

- Delivered Ispin's letter and the Robe of Useful Items to the party.
- Evacuated patrons during the fight.
- Remained to protect his establishment.

## Relationship to Party

**Friendly.** Honored Ispin's trust. Showed courage. A reliable ally.
