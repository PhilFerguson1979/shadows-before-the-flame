# Tanis Half-Elven

**Role:** Companion Leader (presumed)
**Location:** [Solace](../locations/solace.md) -- [Inn of the Last Home](../locations/inn-of-the-last-home.md)
**First Encountered:** Session 1

---

## Appearance

A weary-looking half-elf with hazel eyes that seem to carry the weight of hard decisions. He sits restlessly, one hand always resting on his sword hilt, his chair angled toward the door as if expecting trouble -- or planning an exit.

## What We Know

Tanis was present at the **Inn of the Last Home** during Session 1, seated among a group of companions including **Caramon**, **Raistlin**, **Goldmoon**, and **Riverwind**. He has the bearing and watchful posture of someone accustomed to leading others, though the party has **not yet formally met or spoken with him**.

## Session 1 Events

- Observed at the inn prior to Hederick's attack.
- His group was present during the bar fight.

## Relationship to Party

Unknown. Not yet formally introduced.
