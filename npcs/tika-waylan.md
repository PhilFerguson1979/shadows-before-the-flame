# Tika Waylan

**Role:** Barmaid at the Inn of the Last Home
**Location:** [Solace](../locations/solace.md) -- [Inn of the Last Home](../locations/inn-of-the-last-home.md)
**First Encountered:** Session 1

---

## Appearance

A young woman with a wild mane of curly red hair and eyes full of weary fire. She moves through the inn with quick, practiced steps -- clearly someone who has worked hard her whole life and doesn't back down easily.

## What We Know

Tika works as the barmaid at the **Inn of the Last Home** under **Otik Sandath**. Despite her youth, she carries herself with a toughness that suggests she has seen more than her share of trouble. She is brave, resourceful, and fiercely protective of the inn and its people.
