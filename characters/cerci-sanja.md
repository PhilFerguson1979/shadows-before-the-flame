## Personality

> *Uses big words. Knowledge-seeker.*

Cerci Sanja speaks with a vocabulary that sometimes exceeds the comprehension of her companions. She is driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge and views every encounter as an opportunity to learn something new about the natural world and the forces that shape it.
