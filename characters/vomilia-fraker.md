## Personality

> *Proud of his work.*

Vomilia takes immense pride in every device, gadget, and invention he creates. His wrist-mounted Fire Bolt launcher is his masterpiece (so far), and he is never shy about demonstrating his craftsmanship. He sees the world as a collection of problems waiting for an elegant mechanical solution.
