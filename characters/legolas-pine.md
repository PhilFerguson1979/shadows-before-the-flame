## Personality

> *Always calm. Freedom-loving. Wants to steal something important back.*

Legolas Pine carries himself with quiet confidence and an unshakeable sense of composure. He values personal freedom above all else and harbors a deep motivation to reclaim something that was taken from him. His calm demeanor masks a determined resolve.
